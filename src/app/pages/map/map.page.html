Welcome to Valencia!

With this application, you will visit the main places of the city. Walk to the point on the map, there is no path so just go wherever you want. Once you arrive at the point, a message will be shown with an image of the place and audio explaining what you are looking at. After that, we will check if you have been paying attention to it with a question about that place. At the end of the IGSM event, team with more correct answers will be priced at the gala dinner.
